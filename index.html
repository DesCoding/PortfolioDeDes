About Me

Desiree continually seeks opportunities to learn and grow. During her eighteen year career at Boeing, she has acquired experience in Contracts & Pricing supporting F-18, F-15, & AV8 spares proposals, Supply Chain Management Order Execution, Asset Management supporting the Harpoon Weapons Depot, Integration for Weapons International Project Management, and F-15 Sustainment Project Management. Desiree obtained a Masters of Management from Fontbonne University (2009), Bachelors in Sociology from Webster University (2005), is a graduate of the Advanced Supply Chain Management Certificate Program through SLU (2010) and the BDS Supply & Operations Chain St. Louis Leadership Development program (2015). Desiree is currently pursuing full stack web development certification through James McKelvey School of Engineering at Washington University. Upon completion of this program, she will be proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, C#, ASP.net, Python, Django, Java, PHP, Laravel, APIs, building web pages from scratch, mastering terminal commands, DOM manipulation, consuming RESTful, parsing JSON to extract meaningful data, AJAX to update website data. Outside of work and school, Desiree enjoys spending time with family, yoga, and volunteering as a Girl Scout Troop Leader encouraging girls to lead with courage and character while striving to make the world around them a better place.
